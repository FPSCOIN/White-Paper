## Dumping & price impact
Let us be clear, 99.9% of new tokens today are facing the Pumping&Dumping, this phenomenon occurs when the first buyers sell as soon as their investment has been doubled or tripled, which creates a massive impact on price since the first days of the listing. Therefore, hundreds or even thousands of euros/dollars drain in a record time, and these ‚Äòfake investors‚Äô generate big and quick profit at the expense of loyal holders.  

__Pumping&dumping__ phenomena can happen because of BOTS or incredibly early sellers. BOTS track weak spots of new cryptocurrency projects in the market, then they take over it. They purchase huge assets when its price is low, and immediately sell everything out again in fraction of seconds. 

As a result, the value of the attacked coin drops low drastically. The same effect is created by early sellers when they purchase a certain number of tokens and sells them right away, in no time. This operation does not only drop the coin‚Äôs financial value, but it also shows a lack of trust in the project and discourage real investors and true holders. 

Another liable scenario to induce volatility and __price impact__, is when someone with huge wallets for instance, decides to suddenly sell out giant shares. And again, the token value drops dramatically, exactly like in the other explained situations above. This price impact problem puts other holders at serious disadvantage, especially if the project developers did not conceive any mechanism to rule out such a drop. 

How easy has it been to provoke several types of immeasurable bulls and runs on so many cryptocurrencies' victims and destroy many projects‚Äô values from their launch?
